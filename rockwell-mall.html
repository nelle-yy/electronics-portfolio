Rockwell Nepo Powerplant Mall

A high-end commercial development requiring full-scale integration of building-wide electronics systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Designed complete electronic infrastructure: fire detection and alarm, PA, CCTV, telecoms, FTTH (fiber to the home), BMS, security/access control, carpark barrier systems, and guard tour systems
  • Coordinated system layout with structural, mechanical, and architectural teams

Technologies & Systems

Fire Detection • FTTH • Public Address • CCTV • Access Control • BMS • Carpark Systems • Telecom Backbone

Outcome

Successfully supported the development of a fully connected smart mall environment with high safety, security, and digital capability.
