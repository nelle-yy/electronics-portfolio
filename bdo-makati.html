BDO Makati – Corporate Headquarters Fitout

A high-end office fitout project involving specialized spaces such as auditoriums, executive offices, ballroom, and chapels.

Key Responsibilities

  • Designed low-voltage systems including fire detection and alarm, public address (PA), CCTV, structured cabling, and access control
  • Coordinated with architects, interior designers, and other engineering disciplines for seamless system integration
  • Ensured compliance with building codes and client specifications

Technologies & Systems

Fire Detection • PA System • CCTV • Security/Access Control • Structured Cabling • BMS (Building Management System)

Outcome

Delivered a fully integrated electronics system that enhanced building safety, communication, and operational efficiency.
