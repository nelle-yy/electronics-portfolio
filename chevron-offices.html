Chevron Offices – Corporate Fitout

Office development project involving global corporate standards and strict compliance for safety, security, and communication systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Managed design and coordination from concept to construction and handover stages
  • Designed and validated systems such as PA, fire alarm (FDAS), structured cabling, CCTV, security/access control, and audio-visual systems
  • Prepared tender documents, technical specifications, and compliance reports

Technologies & Systems

FDAS • Public Address • CCTV • Access Control • Structured Cabling • AV Systems

Outcome

Delivered a compliant, high-performance electronics infrastructure aligned with global corporate standards.
